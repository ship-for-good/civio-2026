Two days, one mission

A hackathon with purpose: helping social organizations and benefiting society. In this first edition, we'll work with Civio, where we'll outline the problems they face every day to achieve their mission. We'll help by putting technology and the new reality of AI-powered development at their service.

Friday 29 May

18:00 – 21:00

Civio presents the real challenges to work on. Teams are formed (you can come with yours or join one). First sketches and networking.

Saturday 30 May

10:00 – 21:00

Full-day work session: dev, data, design and product running in parallel. Profile-specific talks throughout the day. Continuous mentoring to unblock and advance. Final showcase of the most ambitious solutions built with AI.
